## Why Convert PDF to Markdown?

PDF files are everywhere. Reports, research papers, invoices, and manuals all live inside PDFs. But when you need to edit, search, or reuse that content, PDF falls short. Markdown, on the other hand, is a lightweight text format that works with almost every modern tool — from GitHub to Notion to ChatGPT.

Here are the top reasons to convert your PDFs to Markdown:

- **AI and LLM compatibility** — Large language models work best with plain text. Feed your documents directly into ChatGPT, Claude, or any AI tool without formatting issues.
- **Version control** — Track changes in Git just like code. Markdown diffs are clean and readable.
- **Universal editing** — Open and edit Markdown in any text editor. No special software required.
- **Documentation systems** — Use your content in Jekyll, Hugo, Docusaurus, MkDocs, or any static site generator.
